# GigsForGigs

> A specialized marketplace connecting early-stage startups with skilled talent for outcome-driven micro-gigs

---

## Problem Statement

Managing the onboarding and profile system of gig workers for a specialized marketplace connecting early-stage startups with skilled talent for short-term, outcome-driven tasks.

**For Startups:**
- Need specific tasks completed quickly (landing pages, pitch decks, product assets)
- Traditional hiring is too slow and expensive for short-term work
- Large platforms like Upwork are noisy, resume-heavy, and often too expensive

**For Creators and Gig Workers:**
- Talented students and professionals struggle with discoverability
- Limited opportunities to build portfolios with real startup projects
- No structured way to establish credibility and convert skills into opportunities

**The Gap**: Startups need fast execution of small tasks with quality outcomes, while talent wants real project work and portfolio building. Traditional platforms fail to bridge this gap efficiently.

---

## Solution Overview

**GigsForGigs** solves the speed versus quality trade-off by focusing on standardized, limited-scope tasks rather than open-ended employment.

### How It Works

1. **Smart Matching**: Startups post tasks; verified creators apply with portfolios
2. **Standardized Workflows**: Clear SOPs from requirement posting to completion
3. **Trust Building**: Mandatory testimonials and referral systems
4. **Specialized Focus**: Platform champions niche services for excellence

## Planned Features

### For Creators / Gig Workers

**Profile & Onboarding**
- Professional profile with skills, portfolio uploads, and public task history
- Quality filtering before platform activation

**Work & Credibility**
- Browse and apply to tasks with portfolio samples
- Well-defined payment cycles with real-time tracking
- Testimonial system and mandatory referral program

**Support**
- Cloud call support for complex issues
- User-friendly interface and comprehensive help center

---

## Key Terminology

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Micro-gigs** | Small, short-term tasks that can be completed quickly |
| **Supply** | Gig workers/talent available on the platform |
| **Demand** | Clients/organizations posting work requirements |
| **Outcome-driven tasks** | Specific deliverables with clear end goals (e.g., "build a landing page") |
| **SOP** | Standard Operating Procedure - defined workflow from requirement to completion |
| **Testimonial** | Reviews and ratings left by clients or creators after task completion |
| **Portfolio building** | Creating work samples to demonstrate skills to future clients |
